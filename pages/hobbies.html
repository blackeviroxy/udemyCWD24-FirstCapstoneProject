A list of my hobbies is given below

Technical and Career-Enhancing Hobbies

  • Participate in coding challenges (e.g., LeetCode, HackerRank).
  • Work on personal projects or contribute to open-source.
  • Experiment with design tools like Figma or Adobe XD.
  • Use engines like Unity or Godot to build simple games.
  • Explore platforms like TryHackMe or Hack The Box.
  • Write articles on Medium or Dev.to about your learning journey.
  • Experiment with TensorFlow, PyTorch, or other AI tools.

Creative and Artistic Hobbies

  • Learn tools like Canva, Photoshop, or Illustrator to complement web development.
  • Explore tools like Procreate or Krita to design assets for your projects.
  • Learn photography and photo editing, which can enhance your web design portfolio.
  • Experiment with DAWs (Digital Audio Workstations) like FL Studio.

Problem-Solving and Analytical Hobbies

  • Play chess, Sudoku, or solve logic puzzles to sharpen problem-solving skills.
  • Tackle complex algorithmic problems or design data structures as a hobby.

Physical and Wellness Activities

  • Stay active with yoga, gym workouts, or a sport.
  • Practice mindfulness to improve focus and reduce stress.

Exploratory and Social Hobbies

  • Join tech meetups, webinars, or communities like Toastmasters.
  • Play multiplayer or strategic games to improve teamwork and reflexes.
  • Attend hackathons, workshops, or conferences in new locations.
  • Learn new programming languages or even a human language.

Practical Life Skills

  • Develop a cooking hobby to balance your screen time with hands-on creativity.
  • Experiment with Raspberry Pi, Arduino, or IoT projects.
